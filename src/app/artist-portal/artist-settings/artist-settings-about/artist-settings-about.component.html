ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Pause upcoming billing cycle

If you pause a billing cycle, Patreon will not charge your patrons on June 1. Billing will resume on July 1, or you can pause again between June 1 and July 1. You may do this as many times as you like. If you have Charge Up Front enabled, new pledges will be charged during this time. If you don't want new patrons to sign up, update your creator page to tell fans you're on hiatus.
